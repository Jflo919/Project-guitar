About Grady


I've been instructing children and adults of all ages since 1991. I hold a Bachelor of Music degree from California State University, Northridge where I studied with Ronald C. Purcell and a Master of Music degree from the San Francisco Conservatory of Music where I studied with David Tanenbaum. I have been a registered Suzuki Guitar instructor since 1998. From 1999 to 2006, I served a Suzuki apprenticeship under master teacher Frank Longay.

"Any child who is properly trained can develop musical ability


just as all children develop the ability to speak their mother tongue."


Shinichi Suzuki
